Clearer: Estimate adverse effect burden of a list of medications

This tool provides a summary estimate of risk for a particular category of adverse effects, based on aggregating adverse effect frequency from a list of prescription medications. Frequencies are estimated based on package insert labeling and postmarketing surveillance. If the 'p450 engine' box is checked, the most common cyp450 drug interactions are also modelled. If the 'use frequency' box is checked, frequencies from labels will be used; otherwise any reported adverse effect is weighted equally, reflecting the imprecision of AE reports.

Disclaimer: This tool is not intended to diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any disease.
